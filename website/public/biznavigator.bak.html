How it works
Four phases. One clear path.

Every Richmond business must complete Legal → Location → License in order. Phase 4 unlocks growth resources, certifications, and funding.

Phase 01
Legal Formation
Register with VA SCC • Federal EIN • State taxes.
STATE • ~$100
Phase 02
Zoning & Location
Live zoning check via Richmond Zoning Map (Atlas.co). Home Occupation Permit or Zoning Certificate.
CITY • $30-$300
Phase 03
Business License
BPOL License from City Finance. Register on SAM.gov and eVA for contract opportunities.
CITY • $30 FLAT
Phase 04
Growth & Support
SCORE mentoring • SWaM certification • SBA loans • MBD certification • Chamber membership.

💻 VITA Statewide IT Contracts
Virginia Information Technologies Agency

Statewide IT contract vehicles usable by Richmond and all Virginia agencies. Cloud, cybersecurity, staffing, software, networking.

💻 Browse VITA Statewide Contracts → 🎍 SWaM Certification (DSBSD) →
How to register on eVA: Visit eva.virginia.gov → Supplier Login → Register Now. Free. Takes 1 business day. Once registered you receive automatic notifications for solicitations matching your commodity codes. Minority/women-owned businesses should also obtain SWaM certification for preference weighting on Virginia state contracts.
