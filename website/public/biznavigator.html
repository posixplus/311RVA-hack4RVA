How it works
Four phases. One clear path.

Every Richmond business must complete Legal → Location → License in order. Phase 4 unlocks growth resources, certifications, and funding.

🏛
1

Legal Formation

Register with VA SCC • Federal EIN • State tax registration.

State Level • ~$100
📍
2

Zoning & Location

Home Occupation Permit or Certificate of Zoning Compliance • Building permits.

City Level • ~$50–$250
📄
3

Business License

BPOL license from City Finance • SAM.gov • eVA Virginia registration.

City Level • ~$30
📈
4

Growth & Support

SCORE mentoring • SWaM certification • MBD Office • SBA programs.

Ongoing • Mostly Free
